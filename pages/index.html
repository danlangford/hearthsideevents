Hearthside Events

Grassroots Competitive Hearthstone • Utah • 2014-2016

Hearthside.Events was a volunteer-run organization focused on growing competitive Hearthstone play in Utah through local events, structured leagues, livestreams, and convention partnerships.

What We Did

  • Tournaments: Local one-day events at stores and cafes with real prize support—including Blizzard Fireside Gatherings and sponsored brackets with prize pools up to $1,000.
  • League Play: Multi-season Hearthside League with structured match play and streamed playoffs, helping players grow across weeks.
  • Livestreams: Live match coverage and commentary via our Twitch channel.
  • Conventions: Ran official Hearthstone tournaments at Salt Lake Gaming Con, including Blizzard-approved Tavern Hero Qualifiers.
  • Community: Hosted casual meetups, deck clinics, and giveaways through our Facebook group.

Where We Hosted

We held events across Utah at venues like:

  • Granato's Deli (Daybreak, South Jordan)
  • The Break Sports Bar (Daybreak, South Jordan)
  • Salt Palace Convention Center (SL Gaming Con)
  • Game Haven (Sandy, UT)
  • Game Den (Provo, UT)

Legacy & Impact

  • Many league seasons completed
  • Dozens of local and convention tournaments
  • Hundreds of dollars awarded in cash prizes
  • Official Blizzard points via Tavern Hero qualifiers
  • A lasting, positive community still active in Utah
